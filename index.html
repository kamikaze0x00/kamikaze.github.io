About Kamikaze0x00

Security researcher, CTF player, and penetration tester

Hello, I'm Kamikaze0x00

I'm a security enthusiast with a passion for Capture The Flag competitions and penetration testing. I've been participating in CTF events for over 3 years, with a focus on binary exploitation, web security, and privilege escalation.

My journey in cybersecurity started when I first discovered the world of ethical hacking. Since then, I've been continuously learning and improving my skills through practice, research, and competing in various CTF events.

This blog is where I share my writeups and solutions for CTF challenges I've solved. My goal is to help others learn and improve their cybersecurity skills by providing detailed explanations and step-by-step guides.

When I'm not hacking, you can find me learning about new security vulnerabilities, developing tools, or contributing to open-source security projects.
